Shanshan Wang (王珊珊)

 

Hi, I am a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Decision Sciences of HEC Montréal (the business school of Université de Montréal), GERAD and CIRRELT since June 2020, where I am working with Professor Erick Delage and Professor Leandro C. Coelho from Department of Operations and Decision Systems of Université Laval. Prior to this, I obtained my Ph.D. in Management Science and Engineering at Beijing Institute of Technology in June 2020, and studied Master in Applied Mathematics at Beijing Institute of Technology in June 2015. During my Ph.D., I visited the Department of Industrial Engineering and Management Sciences and Professor Sanjay Mehrotra at Northwestern University from Sep. 2017 to Sep. 2019, and the Department of Decision Sciences of HEC Montréal from Sep. 2019 to May 2020.

Research areas:
Data-driven decision-making under uncertainty
Integrated Machine Learning and Mathematical Optimization
decomposition scheme for large-scale MILP
healthcare operations management
urban transportation and logistics
